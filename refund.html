Refund Policy

At EliteTresses, we are committed to your satisfaction and strive to provide high-quality hair products and accessories. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we’re here to help.

1. Returns and Refunds Eligibility

You have 14 calendar days from the date you receive your order to request a return.

To be eligible for a refund, the item must be:

  • Unused, unworn, and in the original packaging.
  • With all tags and labels attached.

2. Non-Refundable Items

  • Items marked as Final Sale or Non-Returnable cannot be refunded.
  • Due to hygiene reasons, we do not accept returns or exchanges on wigs, hair extensions, or any product that has been used or altered.

3. How to Request a Refund

Contact our Customer Support team within 14 days at support@elitetresses.com with:

  • Your order number
  • Reason for the return
  • Photos if the item is defective or damaged

We will provide instructions on how to return the product.

4. Return Shipping

  • Customers are responsible for the cost of return shipping unless the item is defective or incorrect.
  • We recommend using a trackable shipping service to ensure your return is received.

5. Processing Your Refund

  • Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.
  • Approved refunds will be processed within 7 business days to the original payment method.
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact us within 7 days of delivery.

Provide photos of the damage/defect, and we will arrange a replacement or full refund.

7. Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. Please follow the return process and place a new order for the desired item.

If you have any questions or need assistance, please don’t hesitate to reach out to our support team at support@elitetresses.com call or message us at 07018814724

Thank you for shopping with EliteTresses — your beauty is our passion!
