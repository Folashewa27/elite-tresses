Shipment Policy

Our shipping process is designed to be swift, secure, and as luxurious as our products. With hubs both in Italy and Nigeria, we ensure your hair essentials travel with care, no matter where you are.

Shipping Locations

We ship across all Nigerian states and internationally — with fulfillment centers in Lagos and Italy.

Processing Time

• Orders are processed within 24–48 hours after confirmation (excluding weekends and holidays). • Custom or pre-order items (e.g., wigs or accessories) may require additional processing time (3–5 business days). — beauty takes time, and we ensure every product leaves our hands in perfection.

  • Standard orders: 24–48 hrs
  • Custom items: 3–5 business days

Delivery Time

  • Within Nigeria: 2–5 days
  • International: 7–14 days

Shipping Fees

Automatically calculated at checkout. Orders above ₦500,000 enjoy free shipping in Nigeria.

Tracking & Support

Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive an email or SMS with your tracking number. You can monitor your shipment every step of the way.
